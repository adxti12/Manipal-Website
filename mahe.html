MAHE Institutions and Facilities

Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT)

One of India’s premier engineering institutions, offering diverse engineering disciplines.

Kasturba Medical College (KMC)

One of India’s top medical colleges, renowned for excellence in education and research.

Manipal College of Dental Sciences (MCODS)

World-class dental education and oral healthcare facilities.

School of Allied Health Sciences (SOAHS)

Offers healthcare-related courses in physiotherapy, audiology, and more.

Welcomgroup Graduate School of Hotel Administration (WGSHA)

Top-ranked hospitality and culinary school affiliated with MAHE.

Department of Commerce (DOCHE)

Offers BBA, B.Com, and MBA programs with strong industry ties.

Manipal Media and Communication (MMMC)

Renowned media school offering programs in journalism, animation, and communication.

MAHE Central Library

A massive resource center supporting all MAHE institutions with extensive collections.

MIT Innovation Center

Hub for research, robotics, and interdisciplinary innovation projects.
