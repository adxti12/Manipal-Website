About Manipal

Manipal, located in the Udupi district of Karnataka, India, is a vibrant university town renowned for its educational institutions and scenic beauty. Originally developed as an educational hub by the visionary Dr. T.M.A. Pai, Manipal has grown into a cosmopolitan town hosting students from all over the world. Its tree-lined streets, lakes, and peaceful environment make it a unique blend of academics, culture, and nature. The town is home to the prestigious Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), and its diverse colleges, hospitals, and research centers attract talent globally. Along with being an educational hotspot, Manipal also offers a lively cultural scene, quaint cafes, temples, and recreational activities, making it an ideal place to study, live, and explore.
